About US


Touch of Bronze Ghana Limited was established in the United Kingdom and registered in England 1979 No. 2394401.

When I reconnected to Ghana the company was again registered in Ghana 1992 as a Limited company. The name of Touch of Bronze evolved during an exhibition of my first works in London.

I was among other artists exhibiting drawing and paintings. A blind man walked through the door and asked to be led to the Art works. He touched a few paintings when he reached my stand he felt one of the Sculptures on my stand. He picked it up and asked, “Am I Touching Bronze works”?

To which I answered, "You are Touching Bronze" - Hence the name Touch of Bronze.

Mr. Francis Kwatei Nee-Owoo was born in 1942 at James Town in the Greater Accra Region of Ghana into a family of talented artists.

He entered the college of Art, at the University of Science and Technology in Kumasi, in 1966. After completing only a year at the University, he left for the United Kingdom in 1968 to furthur his career as an artist.

Struggling to determine which of the Arts to pursue in the 1970's, Nee-Owoo met and briefly worked with the world famous sculpter, Giovanni Schoeman; who spotted his immense talent and encouraged him to develop his own style.

He has experimented with many mediums including industrial materials, chemicals and metal powders. The Cold Casting Technique and finish, which Nee-Owoo developed, is unique to himself.
